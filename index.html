Bio

AJ Vitanza is a 21-year-old pop artist blending slick commercial appeal with undeniable charisma. Born and raised in Buffalo, NY, AJ carries his hometown pride into everything he does — from his relentless work ethic to his vivid storytelling.

Signed to Handmade Records in May 2025, AJ has quickly developed into one of the most exciting new voices in alt-pop, growing his Spotify listeners from 30K to over 525K in just a few months.

His sound is dynamic, adrenaline-charged, and packed with main-character energy — confident yet approachable, built on clean hooks, smooth toplines, and melodies that feel big and emotional.
